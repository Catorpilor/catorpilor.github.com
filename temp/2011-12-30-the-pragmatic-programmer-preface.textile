Whilst reading this book the 2 basic tips for becoming a pragmatic programmer that shared by a lots.

1.Care about your craft.

2.Think!About your work.

In order to be a Pragmatic Programmer,we're challenging you to think about what you're doing while you're doing it. This is not a one-time audit of current practices-it's an ongoing critical appraisal of everyday decision you make,every day,and on every development. Never run on auto-pilot.Constantly be thinking,critiquing your work in real time.
